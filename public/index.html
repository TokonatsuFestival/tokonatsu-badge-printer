Setup Instructions

1. Connect Your Printer

Ensure your printer is connected via USB and powered on.

2. Install Printer Drivers

Make sure your printer drivers are installed and the printer appears in your system's printer list.

3. Discover Printers

Use the "Discovery" tab to find and connect to your printer.

Troubleshooting

Printer Not Found

  • Check USB connection
  • Verify printer is powered on
  • Ensure printer drivers are installed
  • Try a different USB port

Connection Failed

  • Restart the printer
  • Check for paper jams
  • Verify printer is not in use by another application
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting

Print Quality Issues

  • Check ink/toner levels
  • Clean print heads
  • Adjust print quality settings in presets
  • Use appropriate paper type
